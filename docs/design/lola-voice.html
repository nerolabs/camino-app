Your browser didn't expose speech voices. Try Chrome or Safari on desktop.

For production, use a neural TTS with a custom voice — ElevenLabs, or Azure / Google / OpenAI voices — directed for Lola: warm, unhurried, a touch lower, with an Andalusian lilt and real Spanish pronunciation on the Spanish terms.

Why audio matters more here than in most apps: your user is often anxious and reading in a second language. Lola reading a scary official letter aloud — calmly, in plain words — is an accessibility superpower a silent app can't match.
